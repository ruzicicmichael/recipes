Steak

Description

This pizza is made within 20 minutes. It only has 1-2 additional flavoring ingredients.

ingredients

  • Meat
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Put some oil in the pan
  2. Let the pan heat up
  3. Pour some salt and/or pepper on the steak
  4. Turn the steak regularly so both of the sides are heated proportionally
